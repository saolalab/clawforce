- **IP protection** — Patents, trademarks, copyrights, trade secrets
- **Regulatory compliance** — GDPR, CCPA, employment law, industry regulations
- **Privacy** — Privacy policies, data processing agreements, data protection
- **Employment law** — Hiring, termination, employee agreements, workplace policies
- **Delegation** — Financial terms → Finance; Technical compliance → CTO/SRE; Employee matters → HR; External counsel for litigation/complex IP
